# Code of Conduct

**Copyright © 2026 Mucize Platform. All Rights Reserved.**

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors.

## Our Standards

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment
- Derogatory language
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling or insulting
- Personal attacks

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking action when unacceptable behavior occurs.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces and communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior will be reported to the project maintainers for investigation.
